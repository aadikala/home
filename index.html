About Our Gallery

At Aadi Kala, we honor the rich tribal art heritage of Jharkhand...

Every piece is one of a kind — no two are alike — and carries the essence of Jharkhand’s tribal culture.

We don’t just provide art to hang on your walls; we provide a UTA document with every piece.

At Aadi Kala, we strive to preserve heritage, empower artisans, and bring authentic tribal artistry to the world.
